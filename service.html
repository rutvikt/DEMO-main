Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in your graphic design services?

Our graphic design services include logo design, brand identity systems, business collateral, packaging design, digital graphics, and more. Each project begins with a discovery phase to understand your needs, followed by concept development, refinement, and final delivery of all necessary files.

How long does a typical design project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A basic logo design might take 2-3 weeks, while a comprehensive brand identity system could take 4-6 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation after understanding your specific requirements.

What file formats will I receive?

We deliver all standard file formats including vector files (AI, EPS, PDF), high-resolution raster files (PNG, JPG), and any specialized formats required for your specific applications. We also provide guidelines on how to use each file type.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes, we include a set number of revision rounds in each project (typically 2-3 rounds depending on the package). Additional revisions can be accommodated at an hourly rate. Our goal is to ensure you're completely satisfied with the final designs.
